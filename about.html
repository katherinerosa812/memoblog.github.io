About Me

I first started learning to code 2 years ago. Once I started practicing with a couple of tutorials, I realized that I had learned some coding before. Years ago, I would customize my Myspace page. What I didn't know then, was that I was learning HTML and CSS! Now I've gone through HTML and CSS courses with Skillcrush, FreeCodeCamp, and now Mimo and can build real webpages from scratch.
